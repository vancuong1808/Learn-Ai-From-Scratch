Property Structure & Physical Features:
- PropertyType: Type of property — e.g., Detached house, Apartment, Mansion, or RowHouse.
- ConstructionMaterial: Main material used in building construction (RC = reinforced concrete, Wood, Steel, etc.).
- YearBuilt: Year when the building was originally constructed.
- YearRenovated: Year of the most recent renovation (0 = never renovated).
- Floors: Total number of floors in the building.
- Orientation: Primary directional orientation of the property (North, South, East, West).
- LandArea_sqm: Land area (in square meters) the property sits on.
- TotalFloorArea_sqm: Combined total floor area of all stories in the property (sqm).
- FloorHeight_cm: Average height of each floor, measured in centimeters.

Safety, Legal & Resilience:
- EarthquakeResistant: Whether the building was constructed or retrofitted to meet earthquake resistance standards (1 = yes).
- SeismicZone: Level of seismic activity in the area (1 = lowest risk, 5 = highest).
- FloodRiskLevel: Already described above; important here for resilience.
- LegalComplianceScore: Score from 1–10 reflecting how well the property adheres to local building laws.
- OwnershipType: Legal ownership category (Freehold, Leasehold_50yr, Leasehold_30yr).

Amenities & Features
- HasBalcony / HasRooftop / HasGarden: Binary flags for presence of outdoor features (1 = yes).
- ParkingType: Type of parking available (Mechanical, Manual, None).
- HasParking: Binary indicator for whether parking is available on the property.
- PetFriendly: Whether pets are allowed.
- InsulationLevel: Level of thermal insulation (None to High).
- HasGym, HasConcierge, HasLounge, HasGuestRoom: Binary indicators of luxury shared amenities.
- SmartHome: Binary flag for smart home integration.
- LightingSystem: Type of lighting system (Standard, Smart, Auto).
- FloorHeating / CentralAC: Whether floor heating or central air conditioning is included.
- StorageSpace_sqm / HasStorageRoom: Space (in sqm) and flag indicating additional storage.

Interior Layout & Rooms
- RoomCount / BedroomCount / LivingRoomCount / BathroomCount / ToiletCount: Count of respective rooms in the property.
- KitchenType: Type of kitchen (e.g., Island, Compact, Closed).
- KitchenQuality: Quality of kitchen materials and appliances (Standard or High-End).
- WasherDryerIncluded / FurnitureIncluded: Binary indicators for included appliances/furniture.
- IsBathAndToiletSeparate: Whether the bathroom and toilet are in separate rooms.

Condition & Quality Indicators
- ExteriorCondition / InteriorCondition: Rated from 1 (poor) to 5 (excellent).
- MaintenanceScore: A broader measure (1–10) of how well the property has been maintained.
- EnergyEfficiencyRating: Energy performance rating (A = best, E = worst).
- AirQualityScore / ThermalInsulationScore / EarthquakeSafetyScore: Scores (1–10) for internal air quality, thermal retention, and earthquake preparedness.

Market & Economic Factors
- MonthlyMaintenanceFee_JPY: Monthly fee (in Japanese Yen) required for building upkeep and shared services.
- LastSoldPrice_JPY: The property's previous recorded sale price in Japanese Yen.
- NeighborhoodAvgIncome_JPY: Average annual household income in the surrounding neighborhood.
- RentalYieldEstimate: Estimated annual rental yield (as a percentage of property price).
- PopulationGrowthRate: Recent growth rate of population in the area (can be negative).
- ForeignerPopulationRatio: Proportion of non-Japanese residents in the neighborhood.
- NumberOfListings_in_Ward_Last3Months: Real estate listing activity in the same ward over the last 3 months.
- AverageDaysOnMarket_in_Ward: Average number of days listings remain unsold in the ward (market speed).
- FloorAreaRatio_Limit / BuildingCoverageRatio_Limit: Zoning limits for development in the area — FAR and BCR are important legal constraints.

Listing & Transactional Metadata
- SaleType: Entity responsible for selling the property (Owner, Bank, or Company).
- SaleCondition: Type of sale (Normal, Foreclosure, Inheritance).
- Availability: Property availability status (Immediate, Rented, VacantSoon).
- ListedBy: Who listed the property (Owner or Agent).
- ListingYear / ListingMonth: When the property was listed.
- AgentRating: Rating of the listing agent (1–5).
- BuildingInsuranceIncluded / LoanSupportAvailable / FirstTimeBuyerEligible: Binary flags for financial assistance or benefits.

Special Flags & Cultural Features
- IsJikoBukken: Indicates a stigmatized property (e.g. previous incident on-site).
- IsOldDangerousBuilding: Flags buildings considered unsafe due to age and lack of earthquake resistance.
- IsAirbnbZone: Property located in area with potential for Airbnb operations (Shinjuku/Shibuya, apartment, small size).
- IsRedevelopmentArea: Area flagged for urban redevelopment (usually commercial and flood-prone).
- NearbyNegativeFacility: Whether there's a facility nearby that could negatively impact value (e.g., waste plant).
- IsLuxuryNearMajorStation: Property meets luxury conditions and is close to a major transport hub.
- IsTraditionHighPrice: Traditional Japanese features (tatami, shoji) and large size — often valued higher.
- TatamiRoomPresent / ShojiScreensIncluded: Traditional Japanese interior elements.
- NearbyShrineOrTemple: Binary flag indicating proximity to a shrine or temple.
- GreenBuildingCertified: Binary indicator whether the property meets green building certification.
- CustomFeature_1 → CustomFeature_5: Synthetic or hidden features to simulate noise or interaction terms.

Target Variable
- Price_JPY: Final property sale price in Japanese Yen — this is the target variable to be predicted.
